Privacy & Compliance Statement

This extension is developed and provided by Sylin Labs for local installation and use in your browser. We value your privacy and data security, and strictly comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and all applicable laws.

1. Data Collection & Processing

  • We access your camera stream only with your explicit permission, for real-time posture detection and local rendering.
  • All detection and computation happen locally; no images or key-point data are uploaded to any server or third-party service.
  • No personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email, location) is ever collected or stored.

2. Data Storage & Retention

  • This extension does not persistently save any video or posture data.
  • Once you close the extension page or stop monitoring, all in-memory data is immediately cleared.

3. Data Access & Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade any data with third parties. All information remains on your device for the sole purpose of providing this feature.

4. Text-to-Speech (TTS)

When voice alerts are enabled, we use your browser’s built-in or Chrome TTS engine to synthesize speech locally without sending audio data to external servers.

5. User Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or export personal data. Since this extension does not store any personal data, no further action is required.

6. Legal Disclaimer & Liability

This extension is provided “as-is” without warranties of any kind. The developer shall not be liable for any damages arising from use or inability to use this extension.
